Meet Christina

Hi, I'm Christina!

I am the stylist behind Morrison Hair Co.

Originally from the Chicagoland area, I now call Tennessee home. Growing up in a family of hairstylists, I always knew this was the career I was meant to pursue. Today, I specialize in customized color, dimensional blonding, and Bellami extensions, creating personalized looks that help every guest feel confident and beautiful.

When I'm not behind the chair, you'll find me spending time with my husband, our baby boy Elliot, and our pup. We are usually planning our next Disney trip, or catching up on reality TV. I can't wait to welcome you into my chair!
